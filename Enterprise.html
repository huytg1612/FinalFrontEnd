BMW

The BMW Group uses a mixed reality design experience to make key decisions earlier in their development cycle. Designers and engineers are able to get a better feel for the customer experience and have enhanced their productivity and iteration process.

The Mill &

Monster.com

Award-winning studio The Mill needed to produce several animation spots featuring a large, furry creature, and they needed to do it fast. With an ingenious rig and Unreal Engine, they delivered everything Monster.com wanted, and more.

As a creative agency that focuses on strategy and branding for real estate, Neoscape utilizes a range of skill sets and techniques to visualize worlds that do not yet exist.

AUTOMOTIVE

VW &

Animech

Animech is a development agency that delivers leading visualization tools for VR, AR and web to their clients. They currently work with clients like VW, GE Healthcare and others to help them deliver best-in-class customer personalization and visualization using Animech's 3D-configuration software.
